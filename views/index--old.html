Who We Are

Sthaapak in Hindi means "the one who molds or the one who lays the foundation".

STHAAPAK lays the foundation of an ecosystem platform, bringing a structure to the large network of stakeholders, and facilitating the employable youth throughout his/her development journey. Through our tech-driven framework, the ecosystem stakeholders are synchronized, invested and focussed towards the individual’s journey.

Our primary investor is TECHCELX, an integrated business acceleration and technology investment firm.

Our missionis not far from the word; at Sthaapak we are in the business of molding India's marginalized but engaged youth into successful employable wordforce through a continous development framework built on a high-end technology platform supporting and sustaining the global skill & employement ecosystem.

Our Visionis to nurture 30 million certified & employable youth by 2030
